Patient Readmission Risk Optimization

Tableau Dashboard: Readmission Risk Factors

Project Overview

This healthcare analytics project analyzes more than 100,000 hospital encounters to identify factors associated with patient readmission risk. Using PostgreSQL and Tableau, I cleaned, transformed, and visualized healthcare data to uncover patterns related to admissions, patient demographics, and clinical outcomes.

The goal of the analysis was to explore operational and clinical trends that may contribute to hospital readmissions and support data-driven healthcare decision-making.

Business Problem

Hospital readmissions are a major challenge for healthcare systems, impacting patient outcomes, operational efficiency, and healthcare costs. This project explores how healthcare organizations can use data analysis to better understand readmission patterns and identify opportunities for improved patient care and risk management.

Technical & Clinical Approach

Processing a large healthcare dataset required extensive cleaning, restructuring, and validation in PostgreSQL to ensure data consistency and analytical accuracy.

Leveraging my healthcare background as a Doctor of Chiropractic, I incorporated clinical context into the analysis to better interpret admission trends, risk indicators, and healthcare utilization patterns.

Key Insights

  • Cleaned and transformed over 100,000 hospital encounter records using PostgreSQL.

  • Applied healthcare domain knowledge to improve interpretation of clinical data patterns.
