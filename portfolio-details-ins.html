Third-Party Payer Reimbursement Analysis

Revenue Cycle & Payer Efficiency

This healthcare analytics project examines third-party payer reimbursement trends over a 10-year period to identify patterns in insurance performance, reimbursement efficiency, and revenue cycle optimization opportunities.

Key Technical Insights:

  • Engineered cleaned views in PostgreSQL to handle complex insurance claim datasets.
  • Analyzed payer reimbursement efficiency to identify high-performing vs. low-performing insurance contracts.
  • Developed interactive Tableau dashboards to visualize 10-year cost trends and payer distributions.

Project Information

  • Tools: PostgreSQL, Tableau
  • Dataset: 10-Year Hospital Financial Records
